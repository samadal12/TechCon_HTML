Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into a globally recognized annual event that attracts thousands of attendees. From humble beginnings, it now features world-renowned speakers, exhibitions, and cutting-edge product launches.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire, educate, and connect innovators across the globe. We are dedicated to fostering the exchange of ideas and promoting inclusivity and sustainability in technology development and deployment.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Simmons

Dr. Simmons is a pioneer in AI ethics and gave a landmark keynote on responsible AI development in 2022. Her research has influenced international policy on machine learning transparency.

Mark Yoon

Mark is the founder of GreenCode and an advocate for sustainable computing. He spoke at TechCon 2021 about the future of energy-efficient programming.

Leila Zhang

Leila is a cybersecurity expert known for developing real-time threat detection systems. Her talk in 2023 highlighted advances in AI-powered security protocols.
